# Code of Conduct

We want this project to be welcoming and productive.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions.
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people.
- No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation that violates these standards.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainer privately.
